Say Hello

Growing up, I was always drawn to the intersection of creativity and problem-solving. This natural curiosity led me to discover UX design, where I found my passion for creating meaningful digital experiences that truly connect with people.

This is a little about me!

Born in the Bronx, Raised in Singapore

My journey began in the vibrant streets of the Bronx, but I spent most of my formative years in the multicultural hub of Singapore. This unique blend of American grit and Asian precision has shaped my approach to design - bold yet thoughtful, innovative yet respectful of tradition.

The beginning of the journey for me as a designer

My design journey started with a simple observation: technology should feel human. During my time at Overseas Family School, I noticed how digital interfaces often created barriers rather than bridges. This realization sparked my passion for UX design and set me on a path to create more intuitive, accessible experiences.

Discovering UX

The moment I discovered UX design, everything clicked. It was the perfect marriage of my analytical mind and creative spirit. I dove deep into understanding user psychology, design principles, and the art of storytelling through interfaces. Every project became an opportunity to solve real problems for real people.

Moving to Atlanta

Relocating to Atlanta for my studies at SCAD was a pivotal moment. The city's rich history of innovation and creativity provided the perfect backdrop for my growth as a designer. Here, I've had the opportunity to work with diverse teams, tackle complex challenges, and push the boundaries of what's possible in digital design.

I'm always excited to collaborate on meaningful projects that make a difference.
